# Footer

`<Footer.Top>`, `<Footer.Top.Header>`, `<Footer.Top.ItemGroup>`, `<Footer.Top.Item>`, `<Footer.Top.ContactLinks>`, `<Footer.Bottom>`, `<Footer.Bottom.Links>`, `<Footer.Bottom.Copyrights>`

## Maximum 6 columns in `<Footer.Top.ItemGroup/>`

`<Footer.Top.ItemGroup />` accepts a maximum of 6 child `<Footer.Top.Item/>`. Beyond that a console warning will be triggered.
The column grid size of `<Footer.Top.Item />` will be auto-adjusted depending on the number of children in `<Footer.Top.ItemGroup>`.

- 6-5 children : 'col-lg-2'
- less than 5 children: 'col-lg-3'
