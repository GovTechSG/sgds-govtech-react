# Card

Cards can be used for headers and footers, a wide variety of content, contain contextual background colors and images

## Base Card

`Card` component consist of `Card.Img`, `Card.Body`, `Card.Link`, `Card.Title`, `Card.Subtitle`, `Card.Text`, `Card.Header`, `Card.Footer`, `Card.ImgOverlay`.

## Customise Card Sub-Components' HTMLElements

Use `as` prop to change the default HTMLElement of Card sub-components when required.
